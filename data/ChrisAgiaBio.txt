Christopher Agia is an Undergraduate Researcher at the University of Toronto. He will obtain his bachelor's degree in Engineering Science, Robotics and Artificial Intelligence from the University of Toronto in 2021. He spent 2019 to 2020 as an Autonomous System Researcher at Huawei Noah's Ark Research Lab, and spent several months as a Software Engineer at Google. He joined the Robot Vision and Learning Lab, Department of Computer Science at the University of Toronto in 2020 where he works on task-driven perception and control for robotic systems with deep learning techniques including deep reinforcement learning, representation learning (graph neural networks), and convolutional neural networks.
