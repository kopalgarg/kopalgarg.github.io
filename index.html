Kopal Garg

I am a masters student in Computer Science at the University of Toronto and a member of the Vector Institute. I'm advised by Dr. Anna Goldenberg.

My research focuses on AI in healthcare, and I seek to develop statistical and deep learning approaches for modeling multi-variate clinical time-series. I graduated from the Biomedical Engineering program at the University of Waterloo. I've been fortunate to collaborate with Dr. Vijay Sankaran and Dr. Caleb Lareau at the Broad Institute, Dr. Rosanna Weksberg and Dr. Sanaa Choufani at the SickKids Institute, Dr. Janet Ronsky and Dr. Tomasz Bugajski at the McCaig Institute, and colleagues from IBM Labs.

I intend to set aside a few hours each week for research discussions and mentorship. If you feel this could be of benefit to you, please book an open slot here.
